Printing a Slide to Fit a Paper Size

You can reduce the size of a slide when you print, so that the slide can fit on a printed page.

  1. Open the document that you want to print.

  2. In Normal View, choose Slide - Properties, and then click the Page tab.

  3. In Layout settings area, select the Fit object to paper format check box.

  4. In the Paper format area, select a Format.

  5. Click OK. The slide is resized to fit the printed page, while maintaining the relative positions of the objects on the slide.
