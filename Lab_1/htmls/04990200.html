Date (variable)

Inserts the current date into your slide as a variable field. The date is automatically updated when you reload the file.

To access this command...

Choose Insert - Field - Date (variable)


To edit an inserted field in your slide, double-click the field, place the cursor in front of the first character in the field and choose Edit - Fields.
