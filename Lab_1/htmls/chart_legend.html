Editing Chart Legends

To edit a chart legend:

  1. Double-click on the chart.

    A gray border appears around the chart and the menu bar now contains commands for editing the objects in the chart.

  2. Choose Format - Legend or double-click on the legend. This opens the Legend dialog.

  3. Choose from the available tabs to make modifications, then click OK.

To select the legend, first double-click on the chart (see step 1), then click on the legend. You can now move the legend within the chart using the mouse.
