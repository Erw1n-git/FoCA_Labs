StylesStylesStyles

Specifies whether to show or hide the Styles window, which is where you can assign and organize styles.

Each LibreOffice application has its own Styles window. Hence there are separate windows for text documents text documents, for spreadsheets spreadsheets and for presentations/drawing documents presentations/drawing documents presentations/drawing documents.
