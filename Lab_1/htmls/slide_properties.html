Slide Properties

Sets slide orientation, slide margins, background and other layout options.

To access this command...

Changing the SlidePage Background Fill

Page

Sets page orientation, page margins, background and other layout options.

Background

Defines a background for a single page or for all of the pages in the active file.

To change the background of all of the slides in the active file, select a background, click OK and click Yes in the Page Setup dialog.
