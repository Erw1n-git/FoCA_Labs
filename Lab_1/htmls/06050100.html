Unordered

Displays different bullet types that you can apply to a list.

To access this command...

Choose Format - Bullets and Numbering - Bullets tab.

Open Styles - Presentation Styles - context menu of an Outline Style - choose New/Modify.

Open Styles - List Styles - context menu of an entry - choose New/Modify.

Bullets and Numbering of paragraphs is supported only in Writer, Impress and Draw.


Selection

Click the bullet type that you want to use.
